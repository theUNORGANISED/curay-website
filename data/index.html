Data Access & Privacy Requests

Submit a request to access, correct, update, or delete your personal data.

Your Rights

If you would like to access, correct, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you, you may submit a request by contacting us at privacy@curay.app.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request:

  • Access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction or objection to certain processing of your personal information
  • A copy of your personal information in a portable format

How We Handle Requests

Identity verification

We may need to verify your identity before processing your request, in order to protect your privacy and security. We will only ask for information that is reasonably necessary to confirm you are who you say you are.

Response time

We will respond to valid requests within the timeframe required by applicable privacy laws. If we are unable to fulfill your request, we will inform you of the reason and provide information on how you may appeal the decision.
