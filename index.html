How it works

Three
simple
steps.

Curay isn't just a wishlist. It's a personal product library — structured around the way you actually discover, save, and buy the things you love.

01

Save from any website

Browse any online store and save products in one tap. Fashion, furniture, beauty, tech — if it exists online, it belongs in Curay.

02

Build your collections

Create curated lists like "Summer Wardrobe," "Dream Apartment," or "Gift Ideas." Organize your product world the way your mind actually works.

03

Track, share, and act

Get notified when prices drop. Share collections with friends. Buy at exactly the right moment. Everything in one clean, considered app.

— 01

Save from anywhere

One-click saving from any online store. No more copy-pasting links or taking screenshots that vanish into your camera roll never to be seen again.

— 02

Curated collections

Build beautiful named lists by mood, season, room, or occasion. "Capsule Wardrobe." "Apartment Inspo." "Birthday gifts." Your product life, finally organized.

— 03

Price tracking

Curay watches prices in the background. Get notified the moment something you love drops — and never miss the perfect moment to buy again.

— 04

Share your taste

Make collections public and share your curation with the world. Or keep everything beautifully private. Your library, completely on your terms.
