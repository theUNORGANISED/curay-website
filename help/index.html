Help

Everything you need to know about using Curay. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out below.

  • You can save an item in two ways.

    Option 1 — Copy & Paste

    1. Copy the product link from a website.
    2. Open the Curay app.
    3. Tap the "Add Item" button.
    4. Paste the link into the URL field.
    5. Select the list where you want to save it.
    6. Tap "Save."

    Curay will automatically pull in the product image, title, and price (when available).

    Option 2 — Share from your browser

    1. Open the product page in Safari or Chrome.
    2. Tap the Share icon.
    3. Select "Curay."
    4. Choose your list and confirm.

    If the product doesn't load correctly, try: removing tracking parameters from the URL, using the main product page instead of search results, or re-adding the item.

  • Lists help you structure your saved products by category, goal, or priority.

    To create a new list

    1. Go to your Lists tab.
    2. Tap "Add List."
    3. Enter a name (e.g. "Capsule Wardrobe").
    4. Confirm.

    To move an item to another list

    1. Open the item.
    2. Tap the options menu.
    3. Select "Move."
    4. Choose the new list.

    You can also:

    • Rename lists at any time
    • Delete lists (items will not be deleted unless you choose to remove them)

  • Yes — you can share your curated lists with anyone.

    To share a list

    1. Open the list.
    2. Tap the Share button.
    3. Copy the generated link.
    4. Send it via message, email, or social media.

    Anyone with the link can view your list (view-only access). You remain in full control and can disable sharing at any time.

  • Some shops block product data or use complex page structures that make it harder to pull in the details automatically.

    If something looks off, try:

    • Re-adding the link
    • Saving a different URL version (without tracking parameters)
    • Navigating to the main product page rather than a search result or redirect

    If the issue persists, feel free to reach out at curay.app@gmail.com.

  • You can delete your account directly in the app.

    1. Go to Account.
    2. Tap Settings.
    3. Select Delete Account.

    Please note: this permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.
